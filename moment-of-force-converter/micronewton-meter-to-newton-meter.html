How to Convert Micronewton Meter to Newton Meter

1 µN*m = 1.0E-6 N*m
1 N*m = 1000000 µN*m

Example: convert 15 µN*m to N*m:
15 µN*m = 15 ×1.0E-6 N*m = 1.5E-5 N*m
